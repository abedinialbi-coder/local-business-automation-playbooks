# Appointment Reminder Template

## Use case

A customer has booked an appointment, consultation, estimate, demo, or service visit.

## Basic workflow

1. Appointment is booked.
2. Confirmation message is sent.
3. Reminder is sent before the appointment.
4. Customer is asked to confirm or reschedule.
5. Team is notified if the customer does not confirm.

## Example message

Reminder: you are scheduled for tomorrow. Reply YES to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule.

## Best used by

- Dental clinics
- Medspas
- Law firms
- Real estate teams
- Gyms
- HVAC and plumbing businesses
- Roofing and solar companies

## Notes

Appointment reminders should be clear and useful. Avoid sending too many reminders too close together.
