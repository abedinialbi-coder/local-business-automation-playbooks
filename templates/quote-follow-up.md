# Quote Follow-Up Template

## Use case

A customer receives a quote, estimate, or proposal but does not respond right away.

## Basic workflow

1. Quote or proposal is sent.
2. Waiting period passes.
3. Follow-up message is sent.
4. Customer is asked if they have questions.
5. Team is notified when the customer replies.

## Example message

Hi, just checking whether you had any questions about the quote or if you would like help choosing the next step.

## Best used by

- Roofing companies
- HVAC companies
- Plumbing businesses
- Solar companies
- Agencies
- Local service providers

## Notes

Good quote follow-up should feel helpful, not pushy. The message should make the decision easier for the customer.
