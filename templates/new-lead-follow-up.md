# New Lead Follow-Up Template

## Use case

A new lead comes from a website form, ad campaign, booking page, referral, or direct inquiry.

## Basic workflow

1. Lead is captured in the CRM.
2. Confirmation message is sent.
3. Team receives an internal notification.
4. A short follow-up sequence begins.
5. The sequence stops when the lead replies, books, or is manually handled.

## Example message

Thanks for reaching out. What service are you interested in, and when would be a good time to talk?

## Best used by

- Local service businesses
- Agencies
- Clinics
- Home service companies
- Real estate teams
- Consultation-based businesses

## Notes

The first message should be simple. The goal is to make the next step clear, not to explain the full offer.
