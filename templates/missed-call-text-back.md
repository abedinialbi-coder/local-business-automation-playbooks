# Missed Call Text-Back Template

## Use case

A local business misses an inbound phone call and wants to respond quickly without relying only on manual follow-up.

## Basic workflow

1. Missed call is detected.
2. Contact is created or updated in the CRM.
3. SMS is sent automatically.
4. Team receives an internal notification.
5. Follow-up task is created if there is no reply.

## Example message

Hi, sorry we missed your call. What can we help you with today?

## Best used by

- Dental clinics
- Medspas
- Law firms
- HVAC companies
- Roofing companies
- Plumbing businesses
- Real estate teams
- Gyms and fitness studios

## Notes

Keep the first message short. The goal is to restart the conversation, not explain every service.

This workflow can be adapted to different CRM and automation platforms depending on the business process.
